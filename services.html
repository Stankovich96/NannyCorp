Child Care

Baby care is our priority. Under this category, we provide you with a nanny whose main aim is to watch over and and take care of your child in your absence. These nannies have been evaluated mentally, physically and psychologically. We guarantee the security and safety of your child(ren) with them. They come with years of experience and have been trained in child psychology and behaviour. This makes them the best fit for you if all you need is optimum care for your wards.

Baby Sitter

NannyCorp does not only cater to the babies but to the growing children also. We have babysitters who will take care of your children in your absence. These include checking on the children regularly, ensuring the children's safety, assisting them with their homework from school, cooking and making sure they eat well, managing the children's time for maximum productivity amongst others. The ever-increasing demand to have a better life and take good care of the family places a huge burden on the parents, especially, the moms. They are expected to be everything to everybody. To be candid, this can weigh them down if not well managed.
